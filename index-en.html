üìö About the Project

This hub brings together seven analytical tools for Foreign Policy Analysis developed by International Relations students at the Federal University of Santa Maria (UFSM) during the second semester of 2025.

Each tool operationalizes International Relations theories (Realism, Neorealism, Liberal Institutionalism) into practical applications, integrating empirical data from recognized sources such as Correlates of War (COW), IMF, SIPRI, and World Bank.

Methodology: All projects utilized generative Artificial Intelligence (Gemini, Perplexity, DeepSeek) as a development tool, demonstrating how AI can assist in creating rigorous academic instruments.
